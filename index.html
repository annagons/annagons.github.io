I am a fourth-year graduate student in the Cognitive Sciences program at Georgia State Univeristy. My research focuses on social learning, including skills such as imitation, tool use, facial motion processing, and emotion recognition. Most often, I sample typically developing children between 2 and 5 years old. I also am involved in collaborative research projects that examine these skills in additional populations, including children with autism spectrum disorder and nonhuman primates.
