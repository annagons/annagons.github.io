I am a fourth-year graduate student in the Cognitive Sciences program at Georgia State Univeristy. My research focuses on social learning, including skills such as imitation, tool use, facial motion processing, and emotion recognition. In the Learning and Development Lab, we sample typically developing children between 2 and 5 years old. I also collaborate on research projects that examine these skills in additional populations, including children with autism spectrum disorder and nonhuman primates.
